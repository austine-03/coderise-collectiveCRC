Meet Nkanyiso

The visionary founder behind Coderise, Nkanyiso embodies the spirit of innovation and determination. Starting with a simple belief that everyone deserves access to quality career services and opportunities, he set out to create something extraordinary.

What began as a personal mission to help friends and colleagues with their CVs has evolved into a comprehensive platform that connects talent with opportunity, builds careers, and creates lasting impact in the professional world.
