Expert CV Writing Tips

Professional insights to make your CV stand out

üéØ

Use the STAR Method

Structure achievements using Situation, Task, Action, Result to create compelling, quantified accomplishments.

üìä

Quantify Everything

Include numbers, percentages, and metrics wherever possible. "Increased sales by 35%" is more powerful than "increased sales."

üîç

Mirror Job Descriptions

Use similar language and keywords from job postings to ensure ATS compatibility and show perfect fit.

‚ö°

Lead with Impact

Start bullet points with strong action verbs and lead with your most impressive achievements.

üì±

Optimize for Mobile

Ensure your CV looks great on mobile devices as many recruiters review applications on phones and tablets.

üîÑ

Keep It Current

Regularly update your CV with new skills, achievements, and experiences. An outdated CV signals stagnation.
